Designed specifically for the Android user,
the MIT Mobile Android app allows for real-time access to essential institute information.
Download MIT Mobile from the Android Market.

  • MIT News keeps the Institute community and the world informed about MIT research, innovation, teaching and more.
  • Events Calendar lets you search or browse through events, exhibits, and the academic and Institute calendars.
  • The real-time GPS shuttle tracker allows you to monitor bus routes and schedule a notification as the shuttle approaches your stop.
  • The searchable campus map pinpoints services and buildings at MIT. Wondering what's in Building 31, or what food is available at Stata? The campus map will tell you and provide an image of the structure.
  • Stellar offers mobile access to MIT's course management system, including notifications of announcements for selected courses.
  • The searchable people directory provides contact information for MIT students, faculty and staff.
  • Emergency Info provides contact information for MIT emergency services, and acts as a portal for any campus-wide emergency announcements.

This app builds on the functionality developed for the MIT Mobile Web which continues to be available for your Android device and most other mobile phones and smartphones.

Download the free MIT Mobile Android app from the Android Market.
