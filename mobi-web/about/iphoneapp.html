Designed specifically for the iPhone user, the MIT Mobile iPhone app allows for real-time access to essential institute information.
Download it now for free >

  • MIT News keeps the Institute community and the world informed about MIT research, innovation, teaching and more.
  • Events Calendar lets you search and browse events, exhibits, and other entries on the Institute calendar.
  • The real-time, GPS shuttle tracker allows you to monitor bus routes and schedule push notification as the shuttle approaches your stop.
  • The searchable campus map pinpoints services and buildings at MIT. Wondering what's in Building 31, or what food is available at Stata? The campus map will tell you and provide an image of the structure.
  • Stellar offers mobile access to MIT's course management system, including push notifications of announcements for selected classes.
  • The searchable people directory provides contact information for MIT students, faculty and staff.
  • Emergency Info provides contact information for MIT emergency services, and acts as a portal for any campus-wide emergency announcements. It can be set to send emergency push notifications.

This app builds on the functionality developed for the MIT Mobile Web, which continues to be available for your iPhone/iPod Touch and most other mobile phones and smartphones.

Download the free MIT Mobile iPhone App now from the App Store.

Note: Some features of the MIT Mobile iPhone app, including locating the user's position via GPS and making phone calls, are not available on the iPod Touch.
